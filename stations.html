Station Data

Pictured on the map are all the Indego Bike Sharing stations. Assuming that bikes start out evenly distributed at the start of the day, this visualization allows us to see how the number of bikes at each station grow and shrink throughout a typical day. Drag the slider to play with the data! You can also mouseover each station to see its name.
